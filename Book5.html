101 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think

"101 Essays" is a collection of Brianna Wiest's most beloved and widely read pieces, brought together into a single volume that explores what it truly means to think well and live consciously.

The essays cover a wide range of topics, including purpose, passion, happiness, cognitive bias, emotional intelligence, shadow psychology, relationships, solitude, creativity, daily routine, and the difference between what we think we want and what we actually need.

Some of these essays were read by millions of people before being published in this collection, while others are appearing in print for the very first time.

Rather than being a book to read once and put away, "101 Essays" is designed to be kept close and revisited whenever inspiration, guidance, or a fresh perspective is needed.

This is not the kind of book you read straight through and set down. It is the kind you keep.
