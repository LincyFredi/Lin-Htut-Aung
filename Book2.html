Axiom

Seventeen-year-old Amrey X has one fatal flaw. She can lie in a city where the truth is law.

For 200 years, the citizens of Axiom have been subjected to a truth serum and forced to take an oath from the age of seven. For ten years, Amrey has conformed.

Until now.

The Axiom Government has discovered that someone is immune and will stop at nothing to hunt them down. Amrey must leave the only home she has ever known, but escaping will come at a cost.

Unsure who to trust, Amrey finds herself drawn to a boy whose wit and charm may be more of a distraction than she can handle. When confronted by a figure from her past, she must face a shocking truth she never expected—and make a choice that could change everything.
